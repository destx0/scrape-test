Which of the following has become the first constitution-literate district in India?
Kollam
Ernakulam
Belagavi
Adilabad

​The correct answer is Kollam.

In News

  • Kerala's Kollam has become the first constitution-literate district in India.

Key Points

  • This achievement is the result of a 7-month-long constitutional literacy campaign. 
  • It was launched jointly by the Kollam Administration and Kerala Institute of Local Administration to create awareness about the laws of the country and the rights of citizens
  • Under this campaign, more than 7 lakh families of the district are being educated on the basic principles of the Constitution.
